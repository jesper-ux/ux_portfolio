About Jesper

Hi! I'm Jesper! I'm a UX-Design student at Nackademin, styding my final year of their UX-design program. I'm based in Stockholm, Sweden. I am a problem solver by nature, and love meeting new people and learning from their experiences.

Since 2015 I have been working with creating streamlined processes for retouch of fashion imagery for Looklet AB, as well as setting up and conducting trainings with their service providers in Asia.

2020, I started studying User Experience Design at Nackademin. In addition to that I have a bachelor's degree in Media Technology from Södertörn University. I chose the UX path since I see the value and need for good and accessible user experiences in our everyday lives - for everyone.
